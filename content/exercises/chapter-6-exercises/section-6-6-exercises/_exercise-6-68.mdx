Let $Y_1$ and $Y_2$ have joint density function

$$
f_{Y_1, Y_2}(y_1, y_2) =
\begin{cases}
    8y_1 y_2, & 0 \leq y_1 < y_2 \leq 1,\\
    0, & \text{otherwise,}
\end{cases}
$$

and $U_1 = Y_1 / Y_2$ and $U_2 = Y_2$.

a) Derive the joint density function for $(U_1, U_2)$.

b) Show that $U_1$ and $U_2$ are independent.

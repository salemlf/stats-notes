A random variable $Y$ has a _beta distribution of the second kind_, if, for $\alpha > 0$ and $\beta > 0$, its density is

$$
f_Y(y) =
\begin{cases}
    \frac{y^{\alpha - 1}}{B(\alpha, \beta) (1 + y)^{\alpha + \beta}}, & y > 0,\\
    0, & \text{elsewhere.}
\end{cases}
$$

Derive the density function of $U = 1 / (1 + Y)$.

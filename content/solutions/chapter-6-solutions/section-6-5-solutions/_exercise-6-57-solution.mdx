$$
\begin{align*}
    m_U(t) & = \prod_{i = 1}^n m_{Y_i}(t)\\
    & = \prod_{i = 1}^n (1 - \beta t)^{-\alpha_i}\\
    & = (1 - \beta t)^{-(\alpha_1 + \alpha_2 + ... + \alpha_n)}
\end{align*}
$$

This is the moment-generating function for a gamma distribution with parameters $\sum_{i = 1}^n \alpha_i$ and $\beta$.

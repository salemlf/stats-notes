$$
\begin{align*}
    m_W(t) & = E \left(e^{t(2Y / \beta)} \right)\\
    & = m_Y \left(\frac{2t}{\beta} \right)\\
    & = \left(1 - \beta \left(\frac{2t}{\beta} \right) \right)^{-n / 2}\\
    & = (1 - 2t)^{-n / 2}
\end{align*}
$$

This is exactly the moment-generating function for a $\chi^2$ distribution with $n$ degrees of freedom.
